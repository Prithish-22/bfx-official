Our Story

The Vision Behind
Build Factory X

Build Factory X was born out of a shared passion for technology and a collective vision between two friends: Prithish Raj T and Ahamed Hussain S H.

What started as a YouTube channel dedicated to delivering high-quality, transparent tech content to the Tamil community quickly evolved into a mission. Deeply inspired by top-tier tech creators, we noticed a significant gap in the market for truly custom, value-for-money prebuilt PCs that don't cut corners.

Today, we are dedicated to spreading awareness about custom PC building and providing gamers, creators, and professionals with the ultimate tools to bring their dream machines to life—without the premium brand tax.

Customer Details

Component Availability

The PC hardware market is highly volatile. Build Factory X reserves the right to modify or suggest alternative component selections based on current market availability. Any changes will be strictly communicated and approved by the customer prior to billing.

Included With Every Build:

Warranty Exclusions

Our comprehensive warranty is strictly limited to hardware failure under normal operating conditions. The warranty does NOT cover:

  • Manual or physical damage (e.g., dropped components, forcefully bent CPU pins, improper cable management by the user).
  • Liquid damage of any kind (e.g., water spills, custom water-cooling leaks applied post-delivery).
  • Damage caused by power surges or unstable electrical delivery at the user's premises.

Disclaimer: Prices shown in the configurator are estimated allocations subject to market fluctuations. Misuse of the platform or submission of fraudulent requests may result in a permanent ban from our services.

/Refund Policy

Refund Policy

Understanding our cancellation and hardware replacement terms.

Order Cancellations

Due to the highly customized nature of our builds, orders become strictly non-refundable once component procurement has started or the assembly process has commenced. Refunds are only permissible if the cancellation request is received and acknowledged before procurement begins.

Transit Damage

Any physical damage sustained during transit must be reported to our support team within 24 hours of delivery. Customers are required to provide continuous unboxing video proof to be eligible for a replacement or damage resolution.

Strictly Non-Refundable Cases:

Build Factory X will not accept returns, issue refunds, or replace components under the following conditions:

  • Systems subjected to manual mishandling, unauthorized disassembly, or improper modifications.
  • Any form of liquid damage, environmental damage, or pest-induced failures.
  • Components damaged due to extreme overclocking beyond manufacturer limits.

Post-Delivery Support

We stand by our builds. Your purchase includes 3 months of on-site warranty support and lifetime technical assistance via our channels. Beyond our 3-month on-site coverage window, standard hardware failures must be claimed directly via the respective component manufacturers (OEMs) using the provided original invoices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Build Factory X.

How long does it take to build and ship?

Typically, it takes 7-10 business days from the moment your exact components are confirmed and procured. We spend rigorous hours on cable management, BIOS tweaking, and heavy stress testing before dispatching from our Vellore facility.

Do you offer EMI or Financing?

Yes! We have official partnerships with Bajaj Finserv and Fibe. You can easily convert your high-end custom PC purchase into flexible monthly installments.

How does the warranty work?

Every BFX system comes with 3 months of direct on-site support and lifetime technical assistance. Beyond the first 3 months, individual component warranties (ranging from 3 to 10 years) are covered directly by their respective manufacturers.

Are the systems plug-and-play?

Yes. We install the OS, update all drivers, flash the latest stable BIOS, set XMP/EXPO profiles, and pre-install requested software. All you have to do is plug it into the wall and start dominating.
